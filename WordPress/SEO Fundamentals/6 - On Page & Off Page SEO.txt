What is On Page SEO?
	- On page SEO its what's happening on your website, what you are doing, what's in your control, Things such as
		: Creating Better content basically on your website, creating and optimizing it for search. 
	- Creating better user experience
	- Improve usage of keywords
	- Improving page speed
	
	
What is Off Page SEO?
	- Of Page SEO is mostly out of our control.
	- Creating backlinks
	- Social Media Shoutouts
	- Social Shares
