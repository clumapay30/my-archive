What is SEO? (Search Engine Optimization)

SEO:
	: The act to get traffic from search engines
		: Google
		: Youtube
		: Amazon
		: Bing
		: Yahoo
		: etc...
		
How do we do this?
	: Make content more relevant
	: We need to have the answer are people are searching for.
	: Our job is find what people are searching for.
	
Increase Authority
	: We need to make sure that our content is popular.

Technical Optimization
	: This is the entire process that we need to do in the background.
	
Help Search Engines To Rank Us
	: We need to help them with presenting relevant content, popular content. And we need to do this in the background of our website, our youtube channel or whicheven platform we're using.
